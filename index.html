About Dr. Morales

For more than 34 years, Dr. Wilson Morales has provided obstetrical & gynecological needs for women throughout Southern California. Since establishing his private practice, Dr. Morales has helped thousands of women in need of prenatal care, gynecological surgeries, pap smears, C-sections, and many other services. He is a board certified Obstetrician / Gynecologist, and has printed numerous research publications.

Dr. Morales earned his Doctor of Medicine degree from the Far Eastern University in the Philippines, interned at USAF Hospital Clark then moved to Chicago, Illinois, completed his Ob-Gyn residency at the Cook County Hospital/ Chicago Medical School in 1979. He became a Clinical Professor for years prior to coming to Southern California to start his Ob-Gyn private practice at the Santa Monica area.
